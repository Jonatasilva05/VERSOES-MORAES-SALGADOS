Rock history

The Music That Revolutionized the World

The Rock and roll, also known as rock'n'roll,
is a musical style that emerged in the United States in the
late 1940s and early 1950s, with roots
in North American musical styles, such as: country,
blues, R&B and gospel, and that quickly spread to the
rest of the world.

The most common instrument in this style is the guitar, always
present in the bands, being able to have a single instrumentalist,
or two with different functions (base and solo guitarist).
